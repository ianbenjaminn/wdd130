Q: What is white water rafting?
A: White water rafting is a recreational outdoor activity that uses an inflatable raft to navigate through rough water, often in rivers.
Q: What equipment do I need for white water rafting?
A: You will typically need a helmet, life jacket, paddle, and an inflatable raft. The rafting company will provide necessary safety gear.
