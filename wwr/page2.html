Q: What is white water rafting?
A: White water rafting is a recreational outdoor activity that uses an inflatable raft to navigate through rough water, often in rivers.
Q: What equipment do I need for white water rafting?
A: You will typically need a helmet, life jacket, paddle, and an inflatable raft. The rafting company will provide necessary safety gear.
Q: What is the minimum age and skill level required?
A: Participants are to be 12 years of age or older, and must be capable of swimming short distances.
Q: Are group discounts available?
A: Groups larger than 6 persons are eligible for a discount on their trip. See the pricing page or contact one of our representatives for more information.
Q: What is your cancellation policy?
A: If you for any reason need to cancel your trip, please advise with 1-2 weeks in advance for a full refund. Any trips cancelled within 7 days before the trip will not be refunded. We apologize for the inconvienience.
Q: How far in advance should I book?
A: Our trips are extremely popular and must be reserved far in advance. Plan to arrange the details of your trip and coordinate with us 3 to 6 months in advance.
Q: What happens in the case of inclement weather?
A: We ride! Just kidding. Inclement weather will be dealt with on a case by case basis, but count on a partial refund or the possibility to reschedule for a later time.
Q: Can I bring my camera or GoPro?
A: Bring valuables at your own risk. We do not assume responsibility for lost or stolen cameras or audio/visual equipment. We offer photo/video services as well if you would like to take advantage of that offer.
